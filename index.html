Liveness Detection Tips

For BLINK detection:

  • Close your eyes fully for about 1 second
  • Then open them normally
  • Do it slowly and deliberately

For SMILE detection:

  • Start with a neutral face
  • Then smile widely showing teeth if possible
  • Hold the smile for 1-2 seconds

For HEAD TURN detection:

  • Start facing the camera directly
  • Slowly turn your head in the indicated direction
  • Turn about 20-30 degrees, then return to center

General tips:

  • Ensure good lighting on your face
  • Remove glasses if having issues
  • Keep your face fully visible in the frame
  • Move slowly and deliberately
